
# 2.5 Design processing and analysis

This sub-process designs the statistical processing methodology to be applied during the "Process" and "Analyse" phases. This can include among others, the specification of routines and rules for coding, editing and imputation which may vary based on the mode of data collection and source of data. This sub-process also includes the design of specifications for data integration from multiple data sources, validation of data and estimation. Statistical disclosure control methods are also designed here if they are specific to this business process.

## Input

- Data coding/Statistical applications
- Approved business case

## Output

- Survey Methodology
- Data validation systems
