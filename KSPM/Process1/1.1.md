# 1.1 Identify needs

This sub-process includes the initial investigation and identification of a statistical need. It may be triggered by several factors such as a new information request or an environmental change such as a reduced budget, action plans from evaluations of previous iterations of the process or other processes that might provide input to this sub-process. It also includes consideration of best practices amongst other (national and international) statistical organisations producing similar data and the methods used by those organisations.

## Input

- New data/information requests
- Reports from previous evaluations

## Output

- New Identified statistical need.
