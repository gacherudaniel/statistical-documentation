# 1.3 Establish output objectives

This sub-process identifies the statistical output objectives that are required to meet the user needs as identified in sub-process [1.2](1.2.md) . It includes agreeing on the suitability of the proposed outputs and their quality measures with users. Legal frameworks and available resources are likely to cause constraints when establishing output objectives.

## Input

- [Outputs from sub-process 1.2](1.2.md)
- Agreement with relevant institutions
- statistical practices

## Output

- Draft of statistical objectives
- Quality measures

## Documentation and Tools
