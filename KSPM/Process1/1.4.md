
# 1.4 Identify concepts

This sub-process clarifies the required concepts to be measured from the point of view of the users. Though the concepts identified might not align with existing statistical standards, including the choice or definition of the statistical and other concepts and variables to be used, it be should be undertaken during design variable descriptions

- Existing definitions and metadata handbook

## Output
