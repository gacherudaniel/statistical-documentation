# Promote dissemination products

Whilst marketing, in general, can be considered to be an overarching process, this sub-process concerns the active promotion of the statistical products produced in a specific statistical business process, to help them reach the widest possible audience. It includes the use of customer relationship management tools, to better target potential users of the products, as well as the use of tools including websites, mainstream media (TVs, Radios and Newspapers), social media, roadshows and community-level dissemination, blogs, vlogs and podcast to facilitate the process of communicating statistical information to users. Assessment of uptake and user satisfaction is done in this sub-process, assessment could be through evaluating website hits, complaints and complements (user reviews and feedback) from users as well as undertaking a user satisfaction survey.

## Input

- Communication strategists
- Media platforms
- Website
- Blog, vlogs, podcasts and press statements
- Published reports both technical and popular versions (key highlight report)

## Output

- Increased uptake of the produced product
- Increased awareness
- Enhanced organizational visibility

## Documentation and Tools

- Publicity and Advocacy strategy
- Communication strategy
- Organizational social media handles
