# Produce dissemination products

This sub-process produces the dissemination products, as previously designed in sub-process 2.1 (Design outputs), to meet user needs. They could include printed publications, press releases, electronic dissemination on the internet (websites) and social media blogs. The products can take many forms including interactive graphics, tables, maps, public-use microdata sets; key highlights media reports, specific target group report, brochures, linked open data, popular versions, audio/audio-visual files and downloadable files. Typical steps include:

- Preparing the product components (explanatory texts, tables, charts, maps, quality statements, etc.);
- Assembling the components into products;
- Editing the products and checking that they meet publication standards;
- Peer reviewing the compiled document. 
- Translation of products to other languages

## Input

- Infographics software
- Authors, editors, designers and peer reviewers
- Printers
- Mapping of dissemination and commutation channels
- Anonymized micro-data

## Output

- Structural design/outlay of the output/product
- Prepared press releases, key highlight reports and analytical report
- Formatted, edited and peer-reviewed products/reports
- Translated reports
- Editing and formatting guide and product colour theme;
- Approved products for release;
- Identified dissemination and communication channels such as newspapers, social media, TV channels etc.;
- Printed publications, printed brochures, banners etc.
