# Supporting Models and Frameworks

This includes the overarching processes whcih are the framework that are needed to support the entire statistical production such as such as Quality Management, Metadata Management and Data management.These include:

- [Kenya Statistics Quality Assurance Framework (KeSQAF)](https://www.knbs.or.ke/download/kenya-statistical-quality-assessment-framework-kesqaf/)
- [Generic Activity Model for Statistical Organizations (GAMSO)](https://statswiki.unece.org/display/gsim)
- [Generic Statistical Information Model (GISM)](https://statswiki.unece.org/display/gsim)
